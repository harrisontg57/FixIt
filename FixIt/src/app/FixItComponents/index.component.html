FixIt Map Service

Welcome to the site for all your broken sign needs and whereabouts. On FixIt Map Service you can create an account to add other unknown broken signs, interact with other users, and see broken signs around you posted by other users.

Short Instructions:
1) To add a broken sign near you, you must have a registered account.
2) Chat messages are sent and recieved on the chat page.
3) When using the interactive map, make sure you drag the red marker to the location of where the broken sign is located.
4) Be sure to click the type of sign that is broken, there are 3 options you can select
